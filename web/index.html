How to use Audible Sales

Sale types

2-for-1 — Use one Audible credit, get two books. Price shown as "1 credit".

Monthly deals — Rotating selection at discounted cash prices, no credit needed.

Daily deal — One featured book per day at a steep discount.

Cash sale — Limited-time cash price reductions on specific titles.

Ratings

Rating — Raw average from Audible reviews.

Bayesian — Adjusted rating pulled toward the global average (~4.2) when a book has few reviews. Stops a book with one 5-star review from topping the list.

Weighted — Rating × log₁₀(reviews + 1). Rewards books that are both highly rated and widely reviewed.

Filters

Genre & Categories — All checked = show all. Uncheck items to hide them. Use Uncheck all then check specific ones to show only those.

Column filters (▾) — Each column header has a filter dropdown for ranges, lists, or text search.

Favourites — Star any book to save it. Use Favourites only to filter to your starred list.

Libation — Load a CSV export from the Libation app to mark books you already own. The file is read locally — nothing is uploaded.

Region — Switch between US and Canada Audible stores.

Theme toggle

Cycles through Auto (follows your OS setting) → Dark → Light → Auto. Preference is saved across sessions.
